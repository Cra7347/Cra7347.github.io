COE 4 Class Generator

by Nurhaci

Number of Classes Generated - Choose how many custom classes you want(Not counting the 2 generated if Overwrite Voice of El and Troll King is checked. Conquest of Elysium can only load a maximum of 75 classes, including the ones in base game.)



- The Troll King and Voice of El have special rituals which are tied into their respective class numbers. This option will overwrite their classes to make functioning random classes for them. Unfortunatly, this comes at the cost of having only one randomly generated class per generation for each.
